# Skincare Website Project Blueprint

## Project Overview
A modern, visually appealing, and responsive skincare website optimized for premium user experience and AdSense approval. The design emphasizes clean aesthetics, rich original content, and transparent business practices.

## Project Outline
- **Design Philosophy:** Minimalist, organic, and trustworthy. Use of soft gradients, deep shadows, and expressive typography.
- **Color Palette:** OKLCH-based natural skin tones and botanical elements.
- **Key Features:**
  - **Enriched Content:** Detailed "Our Story" section with ethical commitments and sustainable practices.
  - **Trust Indicators:** Dedicated Privacy Policy and Terms of Service accessible via modals.
  - **Bilingual Excellence:** Full English/Korean support for all sections, including placeholders and policies.
  - **AdSense Ready:** Integrated ad placements, account verification meta tags, and `ads.txt`.
  - **Dynamic Elements:** Interactive product cards and smooth fade-in animations to show site vitality.
- **Accessibility:** Following A11Y standards for inclusivity.
